About me

Welcome to my personal website! My name is Alma Liezenga and I am a data enthusiast from the Netherlands. This website gives a brief overview of my professional background, projects I have worked on, and blog posts I've written.

I have previously studied a major in Information Sciences (B.Sc.) with a minor in Artificial Intelligence at Utrecht University (2016-2019). After a gap year to take on a board position at my student association, I followed the master's program in Engineering & Policy Analysis at TU Delft (2020-2022). I complemented this degree with a specialisation in Machine Learning at Lund University (Sweden) and a graduation project with Spare Labs Inc. on the impact of on-demand transit on access equity in the Minneapolis-St.Paul metropolitan area (Minnesota, USA).

After completing my education I took a job at Trunkrs as a junior data scientist. My responsibilities there included forecasting, GIS, and machine learning projects. After visiting the Phillipines to meet and work with our colleagues there, one colleague published an article profile of Rom and me. Simultaneously, I started volunteering with Fruitpunch AI, their AI for good projects offer a compelling addition to my usual, more commercial, work.
